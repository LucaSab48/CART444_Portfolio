Project description

un|natural is a short video about nature growing through constructed spaces and being warped by continuous urbanism. It uses Yoshihisa Hirano's In the Palace (Agitato) to heighten a sense of urgency.

All footage was recorded by me in Montreal. The piece is inspired by Godfrey Reggio's experimental film Koyaanisqatsi.
